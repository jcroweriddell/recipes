Ingredients

1. Pizza dough
2. Tomato sauce (see sauce.txt)
3. Vegan cheese
4. Yellow peppers
5. Mushrooms
6. Tempeh

Intructions

1. Spread dough in pizza pan. Toss it a few times first if you can.
2. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
3. add delicious cheese
4. Bake pizza for 20 minutes in the oven
4b. let pizza cool 
5. EAT!
